Dr Jie Jiang

Research Fellow · University of Surrey · UK

jie.jiang@surrey.ac.uk

Jie Jiang is a Research Fellow at University of Surrey. She is currently working on the HomeSense project, a collaboration between Centre for Research in Social Simulation (CRESS) and 5G Innovation Centre (5GIC) to study how digital sensors can facilitate social research on home activities. Prior to joining University of Surrey, Jie visited Professor Jeremy Pitt at Imperial College working on the design and verification of norm-governed multi-agent systems and their application to the digital economy and intelligent systems.
